# Privacy Policy

EzMemo is designed as a local-first voice memo and note-taking app.

## Data Storage

EzMemo stores your notes, recordings, images, transcripts, and Markdown files locally on your device by default.

## AI Provider

If you enable AI features, EzMemo may send your transcript text or OCR text to the AI provider you configure, such as DeepSeek, Kimi, Zhipu, or another OpenAI-compatible API service.

EzMemo does not provide its own AI server by default. API keys are configured by the user.

## API Keys

Your API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain. EzMemo does not upload your API keys to the developer’s server.

## Recordings and Images

Audio recordings and imported images are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

## Data Collection

EzMemo does not collect, sell, or share your personal notes, recordings, images, or API keys.
