## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I use AI features?

Open EzMemo Settings, configure one or more supported AI providers, save your API keys, and select the provider you want to use.

### Where are my notes stored?

EzMemo is designed as a local-first app. Notes and related content are stored locally on your device by default.

### Does EzMemo upload my API key?

No. API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain and are not uploaded to the EzMemo developer's server.

### Why did AI processing fail?

Please check your API key, network connection, account balance, and the availability of your selected AI provider. Your original recording or note will be preserved when possible.

### Can I recover a deleted note?

Deleted notes can be recovered from Recently Deleted unless they have been permanently deleted.
